Who We Are

A Registered Non-Profit Rooted in Compassion

Founded on September 23, 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla (Founder & CEO), InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 non-profit organization licensed by the Central Government, headquartered in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh.

With 80G & 12A certifications ensuring transparency and tax-exempt benefits for donors, CSR-1 registration for corporate partnerships, and NITI Aayog alignment with national development goals — we operate with the highest standards of governance and accountability.

Our IAF ISO 9001:2015 certification reflects our unwavering commitment to quality in every initiative we undertake, from food distribution to skill development.

Our Initiatives

Six Projects,
One Mission

Every project is a thread in the fabric of a more compassionate, empowered, and sustainable India.

📚
Project BachpanSala
Education for All
Ensuring quality education for underprivileged children — nurturing young minds to build bright, confident futures. We run learning sessions, provide study materials, and mentor students in underserved communities.
🎯 Impact: Free education access for hundreds of children
👩‍🎓
Project Udaan
Women Empowerment
Soaring towards a brighter future — Project Udaan empowers women through skill development, vocational training, and pathways to financial independence, transforming lives one woman at a time.
🎯 Impact: Skill training & livelihood programs for women
🍱
Project Sewa
Food & Clothing Drives
Serving humanity with compassion — Sewa provides food and clothing to those in need. Regular distribution drives ensure that no one in our community goes hungry or unclothed.
🎯 Impact: Thousands fed & clothed across communities
🐾
Project Jeev
Animal Welfare
Empowering lives, spreading compassion — Project Jeev focuses on animal welfare through rescue operations, shelter support, medical care, and regular feeding drives for stray and injured animals.
🎯 Impact: Hundreds of animals rescued & cared for
🌿
Project Prakriti
Environmental Conservation
Plant for a better tomorrow — Prakriti drives environmental consciousness through tree plantation campaigns, waste reduction, water conservation events, and sustainability awareness programs.
🎯 Impact: Thousands of saplings planted, awareness campaigns
💼
Project Vikas
Skill Development
Enhancing employability and building futures — Project Vikas runs comprehensive skill development programs that equip youth with practical competencies to thrive in today's competitive job market.
🎯 Impact: Youth trained for real-world employment
