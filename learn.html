FAQ

Questions Answered

Do I need prior experience to join LearnSpark?
+
Not at all. We have courses starting from absolute zero for every subject — from "What is code?" to advanced AI engineering. Our AI Tutor assesses your starting level and builds a path accordingly. Complete beginners make up 40% of our learner base.
Are the certificates recognised by employers?
+
Yes. LearnSpark certificates are blockchain-verified and recognised by 500+ hiring partners including major IT firms, startups, and MNCs. Many of our certificates also align with official exam curricula (AWS, Google, Microsoft) so they complement official certifications.
Can I access courses on my phone?
+
Absolutely. LearnSpark is fully mobile-optimised and available as a native iOS and Android app. Pro subscribers can also download lessons for offline viewing — perfect for commutes or areas with unreliable internet.
What language is the content available in?
+
Core content is in English, but our AI Tutor responds in 12 languages including Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Bengali, Marathi, Kannada, and more. We are actively adding regional-language course versions throughout 2024.
How does the 7-day free trial work?
+
Sign up for Pro with your card details — you won't be charged for 7 days. If you're not satisfied, cancel any time before day 7 and you owe nothing. After 7 days, ₹499/month is billed automatically until you cancel.
Can companies buy licences for employee training?
+
Yes — our Team plan starts at 5 users at ₹299/user/month and includes an admin dashboard, team progress analytics, and custom learning tracks. Reach out via our Contact Sales page for demo and bulk pricing.

About LearnSpark

Built by Learners,
for Learners

LearnSpark was founded in 2021 by a team of educators, engineers, and career coaches who were frustrated with how slow and generic online learning had become. We believed that every person deserves a personalised tutor — not just those who can afford one.

We built an AI from the ground up, trained not just on educational content but on how real learners think, struggle, and succeed. The result: a platform that actually adapts to you, not the other way around.

Today we serve 120,000+ learners across 40 countries, with a team of 80 people passionate about democratising quality education. Every feature we build starts with one question: does this help a learner grow?
